welcome to the pro tennis!

Pro Tennis Oman is a source of sporting excellence and a destination of choice for health, fitness and wellbeing. We aim to enrich the lives of the communities and members which we serve by providing innovative programmes and employing inspirational and high experienced people. Since 2009 Pro Tennis Oman offers fantastic programs with a large variety of activities. We are well recognized in Oman with a high level of tennis experience delivering coaching to both the local community and also to students from all over the world. All of our coaches are licensed or registered eithter with PTR (Professional Tennis Registry) or the USTA (United States Tennis Association).Our goal is to make children enjoy tennis in a fun, safe and challenging environment to reach their full potential on and off court. We provide a welcoming tennis environment offering tennis to all ages and abilities. If you are looking for a friendly yet competitive tennis school in Muscat, Pro Tennis Oman could be the perfect club for you. If you need any further information about the club or our membership packages please contact us.

Pro Tennis Team is made up of enthusiastic coaches wanting to develop tennis in the Sultanate of Oman. Our combined coaching experience is the best in the country with more than 35 years of experience under our belt. The team has extensive coaching experience in Europe, USA and Middle East.

We aim to provide you with a fast turn-around Stringing Service with the best quality and with the latest available strings from many top manufacturers, such as Babolat, Luxilon, Technifibre and Yonex. We can also supply and fit racket accessories including racket grips and dampers.
